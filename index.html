I am a 19-year-old student at Universitas Teknologi Yogyakarta with a deep passion for photography and videography. My interest in this field began when a friend invited me to try it out, which unexpectedly led me to a fascinating and challenging journey.

Since then, this hobby has become an essential part of my life. Armed with a second-hand camera from my sibling, I started capturing various moments, from landscapes and portraits to simple everyday scenes filled with meaning.


Over the past year, I have been exploring this hobby through self-directed learning, using resources such as online tutorials, discussion forums, and hands-on experiments in the field. Although I am relatively new to photography and videography, I firmly believe that with dedication and hard work, I can develop my skills to a professional level.


As a proactive individual, I am always eager to learn new things, especially those aligned with my interests. I also possess skills in photo and video editing, which add value to my creative process. For me, every step, from capturing the images to the editing phase, is a valuable experience that continually teaches me something new.


In addition to this, I enjoy playing video games in my spare time. This activity is not just a form of entertainment but also a source of inspiration that often helps me generate fresh ideas for visual concepts. I believe that having diverse hobbies and interests can complement one another and open up greater opportunities to create original and meaningful works.


With a strong desire to continue learning and growing, I aspire to contribute significantly to the world of photography and videography by creating works that are not only visually stunning but also carry a powerful story and message.
